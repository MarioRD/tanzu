What is TanZu?
TanZu is a closs-platform app (iOS and Android) allowing any international student to get hired in China Mainland.
Yes you heard right, in China!!
So why would I want to go work there?
Great question. Well first off let's say that the chinese population is growing by the day and raising the local economy. Cities like Shenzhen have gone from small cities to huge metropolis in a couple of years.
Ok so why would you care?
Well that is the main point, not only would you have the opportunity to explore such a diverse and beautiful environment, you would actually get a chance to give a huge boost to your career by being protagonist of tomorrow's future.
As a company, how can I hire talents?
No need to write to us, we made the app in place for the purpose of connecting YOU to the students.
Just signup as an Employer, post a job and start swiping our talents to schedule an interview.

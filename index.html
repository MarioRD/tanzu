If you can't find an answer here, please contact us

What is TanZu?
Tanzu is a portal for Chinese internationals to reach the best jobs back in China! We empower you to apply to a job in three clicks as opposed to three days!
So why would I want to go work there?
The Chinese economy is booming right now and there is a high demand for internationally educated talent to return. There are thousands of jobs in technology, finance, and marketing in China and we want to help fill these roles with the best international talent.
As a company, how can I hire talents?
The employer can sign up to our platform, post a job, and start swiping through a tailored portfolio of candidates. If they swipe on candidate and candidate has swiped on them, then they will receive a match! Now employers can schedule an interview directly through WeChat.
